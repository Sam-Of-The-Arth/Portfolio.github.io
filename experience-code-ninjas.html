Overview

As a tutor at Code Ninjas, I taught students ages 7-16 foundational programming concepts and hands-on robotics. I worked one-on-one and in small groups, guiding students through debugging, project development, and problem-solving in a learning environment.

Beyond individual tutoring, I took initiative to design and launch a brand-new 3D Printing and Modeling Camp, introducing students to CAD software, 3D design principles, and the joy of bringing digital designs to life.
